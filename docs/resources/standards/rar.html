What is RAR (Rich Authorization Requests)?

RAR extends OAuth to request fine‑grained, structured permissions (authorization details) rather than broad scopes.

Why it matters

  • Enables least‑privilege access aligned to business objects
  • Improves auditability by capturing intent at consent time
  • Pairs well with PDP decisions for runtime enforcement

How it works (at a glance)

  1. Client sends authorization_details describing the requested access
  2. AS obtains consent and issues a token bound to those details
  3. RS enforces the requested permissions; PDP can further constrain
