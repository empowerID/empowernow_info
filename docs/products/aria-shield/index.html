Keep tokens out of the browser. Enforce policy on every call.

An application-aware BFF that brokers tokens per service, evaluates PDP decisions, applies stream-time caps, and emits signed receipts.

For: Enterprise SaaS, Regulated, Platform Teams

Why it wins

Zero-token SPA sessions with HTTP-only cookies
Per-route AuthZEN decisions with constraints & obligations
Budget holds, stream caps, and cryptographic receipts

How it works

  1. Edge authenticates SPA with HTTP-only cookies, not tokens.
  2. BFF validates session and requests PDP decision per route.
  3. On allow, BFF brokers per-service tokens and applies constraints.
  4. Receipts are signed and chained; budgets settle to actuals.
