ARIA Shield

Provable guardrails for AI agents and LLMs. Stop overspend and data leaks with schema pins, plan contracts, stream‑time enforcement, and signed receipts.

Watch 10‑minute demo See Standards Read the tech

How ARIA works

  • IdP mints ARIA Passport (pairwise user↔agent, schema pins, budget)
  • Gateway verifies pins, enforces plan step, checks PDP
  • BFF caps tokens during streaming and settles on usage
  • Every decision emits a signed, hash‑chained receipt

Seven controls — shipped in v1

Schema pins

Version/hash pins for every tool with safe rollout window. Gateway denies on drift.

Plan contracts

Signed steps bind tool, parameters, and per‑step limits. Prevents param drift.

Stream‑time caps

BFF clamps tokens and truncates responses in real time—no best‑effort.

Egress allowlists

Policy pins outbound hosts and re‑checks redirects before following.

Prompt & params guard

Disallowed phrases, parameter allowlists, and server‑side data scopes.

Budgets that hold & settle

Idempotent holds before call; settle on usage. 402 for unaffordable runs.

Receipts as ground truth

Signed JWS with policy snapshot and hashes; chain continuity in Redis.
