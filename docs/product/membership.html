Membership Graph (Neo4j)

Authoritative graph for delegations, capabilities, budgets/max_steps, and SaaS eligibility. Powers both issuance (IdP) and decisions (PDP).

PIP endpoints (read‑only)

GET /membership/v1/capabilities?user_id={id}&agent_id={id}
GET /membership/v1/delegations?user_id={id}&agent_id={id}&status=active
GET /membership/v1/data-scope?subject_id={id}&resource_type={type}
GET /membership/v1/step-up?subject_id={id}
GET /membership/v1/chain-eligibility?user_id={id}&agent_id={id}&tool_id={id}

How PDP and IdP use it

  • PDP enriches evaluations with data_scope, step_up, identity_chain, and verifies capability
  • IdP verifies delegation and fetches budget/max_steps to embed in the Passport

Why it matters

A single source of truth reduces drift between issuance and enforcement. Graph queries keep constraints grounded in real relationships and are safe to cache.
