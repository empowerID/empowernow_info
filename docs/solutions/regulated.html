Regulated Industries

Provable controls on identities, data access, and changes for AI agents.

The problem

Regulations require evidence of control. AI agents raise risk without standardized authorization and audit.

The ARIA approach

  • Pairwise subjects and optional DPoP at issuance
  • AuthZEN decisions enriched by Membership graph
  • Data governance via data_scope and step_up
  • Boundary enforcement: schema/params/egress, plan/budgets
  • Cryptographic receipts per permit

Outcomes

  • Consistent, explainable policy decisions
  • Enforced data boundaries and step‑up
  • End‑to‑end, verifiable audit trails
