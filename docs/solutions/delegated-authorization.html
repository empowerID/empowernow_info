Delegated Authorization

Model permissions in a membership graph, evaluate in a central PDP, and enforce at the edge with constraints and obligations.

Explore PDP Explore Membership Watch demo

What it delivers

  • Permit with constraints (filters, field masks, caps)
  • Obligations: consent, receipts, alerts
  • Relationship‑aware policy via membership graph
  • Consistent decisions across APIs and agents

Outcomes

Least privilege

Constrain reads/writes to exactly what policy allows.

Fewer incidents

Obligations and deny paths reduce risky access by default.

Consistent control

Same decisions across microservices and AI agents.

How it works

  1. Model relationships (who can act on what) in the membership graph.
  2. Services call the PDP with subject, resource, action, and context.
  3. On allow, PDP returns constraints and obligations for enforcement.
  4. PEPs (Gateway/BFF) enforce; receipts capture policy and usage.
