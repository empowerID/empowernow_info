Zero‑Token SPAs

Keep tokens out of the browser. Enforce policy on every call. Our BFF pattern gives you session UX, PDP authorization, stream‑time controls, and signed receipts—without slowing teams.

At a glance

  • HTTP‑only cookie sessions; no tokens in localStorage
  • ForwardAuth at the edge; BFF validates, PDP authorizes
  • Stream‑time enforcement and spend control
  • Signed, hash‑chained receipts for auditability

Why it wins

Eliminate token theft

No browser tokens, no replay. Sessions + PDP keep control at the edge.

Policy on every call

Resource‑level authorization with rich context from the BFF.

Audit‑ready proof

Receipts capture policy snapshots and usage with tamper‑evident chains.

How it works

  1. Edge calls BFF for ForwardAuth; SPA never sees OAuth tokens.
  2. BFF validates session, requests a PDP decision per route/action.
  3. On allow, BFF brokers per‑service tokens and applies constraints (tokens, egress, prompt rules).
  4. Receipts are signed and chained; budgets hold and settle on usage.
