What we enforce

  • Budget holds before execution; settle on usage
  • Stream‑time token/byte caps with hard clamps
  • Provider/model routing based on policy + price
  • Signed, hash‑chained receipts for every call

Outcomes

Predictable costs

Budgets gate calls; caps prevent runaway tokens and large responses.

Provable usage

Receipts provide tamper‑evident proofs for chargebacks and audits.

Faster delivery

Teams ship faster with guardrails; no manual usage policing.

How it works

  1. BFF requests a PDP decision per call; constraints include token caps and budget ceilings.
  2. On allow, BFF applies caps at stream‑time and places a hold for the maximum allowed spend.
  3. After completion, usage is measured and the hold is settled to actuals.
  4. Every call emits a signed receipt with policy snapshots and usage totals.
